# Self Weight

The self weight of a structure is the total force resulting from its own mass and any permanent components attached to it. The self weight plays a central role in floating and submerged systems because it has a direct impact on the other static loads. 

:::{card} Definition
For a body of mass _m_ the self weight is:
$
W = mg
$
