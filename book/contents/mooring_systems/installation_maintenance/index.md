# Installation and Maintenance


This section will discuss the installation and maintenance of moorings systems and their cost. 

Based on this section you will obtain a broad understanding of the main installation equipment used to install mooring systems for Floating Offshore Platforms and understand the cost components of mooring systems. Note that the methods indicated are based on the installation of offshore platforms in the past while for FOW often different methods may be used.
